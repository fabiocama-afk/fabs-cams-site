About me

Finding beauty
in everyday moments

I'm Fabs — a photographer and visual storyteller based in London. My work is drawn to the interplay of natural light, urban texture, and the unguarded human moment.

With a background spanning editorial portraiture, documentary, and architectural photography, I bring a considered and unhurried eye to every frame.

Available for commercial commissions, editorial work, and personal projects worldwide.
